It’s a dangerous business...

Posted on April 11, 2011

We have been working for more than a month now without any online presence.

That is a great sign, since without any advertising we have been able to land a really interesting project. One of those that can truly change the world. Its name is Valioo and it is still in private beta, but you can learn more and reserve your position in the queue.

Anyway, I digress.

The only purpose of this post is to announce (to no one, really) that we have started this wonderful adventure called 1uptalent. This is what we’d call our MVP (or Minimum Viable Product) for a corporate website. There’s still a lot of work to do, but let’s walk one step at a time.

It’s a dangerous business, Frodo, going out your door. You step onto the road, and if you don’t keep your feet, there’s no knowing where you might be swept off to.

We have been extremely well accompanied so far with people like the Tetuan Valley crew (who taught us a lot about startups) or Byron Standford (of Project Presentation fame). We hope to continue walking with them in the future.

See you at the next crossroad! We’d like to walk with you until your next stop…
